Shri Balaji Institute is one of India’s leading Distance Education Consultation and Distance Learning Centers that provide high quality, industry-relevant distance educational courses, programs for students, young graduates, and working professionals.

    We provide admission interface for students seeking admission into regular courses of top colleges/universities.

    • MEDICAL COURSES: MBBS, BAMS, BHMS, BDS, Paramedical Courses
    • ENGINEERING COURSES: B. Tech, M. Tech
    • MANAGEMENT COURSES: BBA, BBM, MBA
    • LAW COURSES: LLB, LLM

    Gaining admission to the best college is an effort requiring competence coupled with enthusiasm, right knowledge and focused endeavour. The first step in this process is undoubtedly the crucial decision as to which college and university best suits your unique needs. We help students in the college application process. With our knowledge and experience, we guide you in the right direction. We have an individualistic approach for every student. We discuss the profile of the aspiring student and guide them with different set of options. Once, the student finalizes on any specific stream then we go ahead with the other formalities.
