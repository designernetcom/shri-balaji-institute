About Graduation in One Year

"Graduation in One Year" is a degree program which allows eligible candidates to complete their degree within one year. It is also searched as “Degree in One Year” over the internet. [Graduation in one year] is a very popular program and highly searched by those who were not able to complete their graduation. Various courses like BA, B. Com, B. Sc, BBA, B. Tech, etc can be pursued in one year under this program.

Benefits of Graduation in One Year

In today’s competitive world, Graduation is a must to secure a decent job with considerable amount of salary. There are many people in India, who had to discontinue their college education owing to various reasons and circumstances. This category includes both, college dropouts and working professionals. They are in constant search of this degree course.

"Graduation in one year" helps college dropouts, to secure a job, who were not able to get one because of incomplete graduation. This program also helps people, already in job, to get an appraisal by completing their graduation.

Is Graduation in One Year Valid?

There is a tremendous confusion over the validity of the program. Aspirants seeking Graduation in one year are in search of proper guidance to clear the uncertainty about the program.

Answer to the above question is: Yes.

Graduation in One Year program, pursued under lateral entry or credit transfer scheme, is absolutely valid. It is approved by UGC, AICTE, AIU & DEB.

How to Complete Graduation in One Year?

Eligible aspirants can get their Graduation completed in One Year either through Lateral Entry Scheme or Credit Transfer Scheme.

Lateral Entry Scheme: Lateral Entry allows candidates with pre-requisite qualifications to get directly enrolled into 2nd year of a course.

For Instance: A person who has completed Diploma Polytechnic can get admission to 2nd year of B. Sc.

Credit Transfer Scheme: Credit transfer allows candidates, who left their education after completing 1st or 2nd year from any UGC recognized university, can complete their remaining years graduation in one year from another UGC recognized university.
