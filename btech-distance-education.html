About B. Tech Distance Education

B. TECH DISTANCE EDUCATION or BE Distance Education is 3 years (Lateral Entry) /4 years undergraduate program that imparts extensive knowledge of different engineering fields and prepares learners for a brilliant career in engineering. Distance B. Tech Degree is offered under various specializations such as mechanical, civil, mining, computer science, aeronautics, electronics, etc. B. TECH Distance education helps to gain industry-ready skills with its industry-oriented curriculum. Distance B. Tech is similar to the Regular B. Tech in respect to syllabus and assessment pattern.

Why B. TECH DISTANCE EDUCATION?

B. TECH DISTANCE EDUCATION is a highly searched distance degree program over the internet. This shows that a huge number of people want to pursue B. Tech through distance education. The reasons may vary behind every need. Distance B. Tech can be a blessing for those who cannot pursue regular on-campus B. Tech programs. B. Tech Distance Education helps working professions to excel in their career, by pursuing further technical education, without leaving their job. Distance B. Tech also helps students, who want to pursue technical education, but cannot continue their college education because of financial constraints and family commitments. Distance B. Tech fee is very low as compared to Regular B. Tech. Most of the people seeking B. Tech through distance education are very much confused about the validity of the degree. They require an explicit answer and proper guidance regarding the same.

Is B. TECH through distance education valid?

This is a question concerning, almost, every individual who wants to pursue B. Tech through distance education. To be very clear regarding this matter “Distance B. Tech is not valid”. MHRD & Joint committee of UGC-AICTE-DEC issued notices dated 29-07-2009 and 13-08-2009 respectively, declaring Distance B. Tech invalid. Although AICTE has come up with an alternative solution i.e., delivering Regular B. Tech through Work-integrated learning programs/ part-time mode/ online mode. There are many UGC-AICTE approved universities in India offering alternatives for B. TECH DISTANCE EDUCATION courses.

Eligibility for B. Tech Distance Education

Students who have completed 10+2 with PCM are eligible for B. Tech Distance Education.

Diploma polytechnic holders are eligible for lateral entry to 2nd year of relevant Distance B. Tech program.

Eligible candidates must be employed or self-employed in order to pursue this course in distance mode.

Fees for B. Tech Distance Education

40K – 60K Per Year (As fees varies according to universities)

Career options after B. Tech Distance Education

After completing Distance B. Tech one can pursue post-graduation degrees like M. Tech, MBA from renowned institutes/ universities like IIM, IIT, Pune University, Mumbai University, etc.

With this degree in hand one can appear for public service commission exams like UPSC, MPSC, etc.

With B. Tech Degree, one join can repute Private sector companies in India & Abroad.

One can also join Public sector companies likes BHEL, NTPC etc.
