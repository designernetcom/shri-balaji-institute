AboutB. Tech Civil Distance Education

B Tech Distance Education in Civil Engineering is a 4 years/ 3 years (B Tech Civil Lateral Entry) degree program that involves the study of building futuristic infrastructures, roads, bridges, dams with the help of science, physics, math and other technologies that can change the future of civil engineering. Civil engineering is considered to be an oldest engineering branch, known to everyone and opted by many as a profession from old times.B. Tech Distance Education in Civil Engineering course is designed, to impart the knowledge and training required, to prepare groomed individuals who will engage themselves in infrastructure construction & development.B. Tech Distance Education in Civil Engineering is a very popular branch as compared to the other branches of DistanceB. Tech. There are several UGC-AICTE approved universities in India offeringB. Tech Civil Engineering through Distance Education/ Correspondence mode/ Part-Time mode/ Online mode.

Eligibility forB. Tech Civil Distance Education

Aspirants who have passed 10+2 in Science with Physics, Chemistry & Mathematics as subjects are eligible forB. Tech Civil Distance Education Course.

Candidates possessing Diploma Civil Engineering (3 Years) are eligible for lateral entry to 2nd year ofB. Tech Civil Distance Education Course.

Eligible candidates must be employed/ self employed in order to pursueB. Tech Civil through Distance Education.

Advantages ofB. Tech Civil Distance Education

Civil engineering techniques are practiced by humans, even, before the existence of very early civilizations. It is an important aspect of day today’s life. The infrastructural/ construction industry, in India & abroad, is flourishing expeditiously. After agriculture, Civil engineering is the largest industry in India. The civil industry offers and will continue to offer loads of jobs & business opportunities.

To start a career in civil industry, from a sophisticated level,B. Tech Civil Degree is a must. Thus,B. Tech Civil Distance Education Degree lends a helping hand to those, who due to high fees and family obligations, cannot take up regular on-campus program. DistanceB. Tech fees is very low as compared to the RegularB. Tech program. DistanceB. Tech Civil course also aids to individuals, who are already engaged in construction industry, to pursue further education in distance mode. It is a convenient option for Civil Diploma holders to complete theirB. Tech course without bothering their current job schedule. Self-employed individuals who want to make their career in construction industry can also be benefitted byB. Tech Civil Distance Education. After successfully completedB. Tech Civil through Distance Education they can acquire “Civil Licence” required for obtaining civil contracts.

Career opportunities afterB. Tech Civil Distance Education

The need for skilled civil engineers is never going cease as infrastructural development & construction will continue till the end of the civilization. The civil engineering industry offers enormous jobs & business opportunities. After completingB. Tech Civil Engineering Distance Education, One can:

  • Start a job as an engineer in private construction, real estate or infrastructure development company.
  • Pursue master degrees like MBA, M. Tech Civil, in reputed and recognized universities, in India & abroad.
  • Start a job as Site engineer, Engineering Project Managers, Environment Engineer, Transportation Engineer etc. in top civil companies in India & abroad.
  • Get a job as Engineering Inspectors, Building Surveyor, Senior Civil Engineer etc. in Public Sector Undertakings like BHEL, ONGC, etc.
  • Join Government Departments like PWD, Indian Railway, Army, Navy, Air Force, etc.
  • Start construction, real estate, infrastructure development business in India & abroad.
