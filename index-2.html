Welcome to Shri Balaji Institute Pune (SBIP)

Welcome to the website of Shri Balaji Institute Pune (SBIP). We stand to provide quality educational services through strategic collaboration with internationally accredited institutions. We are committed to deliver best distance education and professional educational consultancy services in India. We specialize in providing personal guidance to suit the individual needs of our candidates who contact-us from all over India.

With more than 6 years’ experience in the education sector, Shri Balaji Institute Pune (SBIP) offers courses from Secondary level to Post Graduation level via regular and distance mode. We assist candidates to choose and enrol from 200+ courses, available in different modes, as per their choice and convenience. As an organization, we serve as a point of contact for the regular and distant education programs offered by various boards and universities.
