AboutB. Tech Electrical Distance Education

B Tech Distance Education in Electrical Engineering is a 4 years/ 3 years (B Tech Electrical Lateral Entry) distance degree program. Electrical engineering, being one of the most diverse field of engineering, deals with the most essential aspect of our life i.e., electricity. Electrical Engineering specifically deals with the, fundamentals and application, of electricity, electronics & electro-magnetism. It accords the knowledge of using natural resources for generating electricity and also deals with the study of transportation, utilization and storage of the generated electricity.B. Tech Distance Electrical engineering trains it’s aspirants, the consolidated application of electricity, electronics & electro-magnetism, to design, develop and maintain various electrical & electronic equipment required in day today’s life such as electric grid, electric motor, medical devices, mobile phones, etc.B. Tech Distance Education in Electrical Engineering is the broadest branch of engineering. There are several UGC-AICTE approved universities in India offeringB. Tech Electrical Engineering through Distance Education/ Correspondence mode/ Part-Time mode/ Online mode.

Eligibility forB. Tech Electrical Distance Education

Aspirants having 10+2 in Science with Physics, Chemistry & Mathematics as subjects are eligible forB. Tech Electrical Distance Education Course.

Diploma Electrical Engineering (3 Years) holders are eligible for lateral entry to 2nd year ofB. Tech Electrical Distance Education Course.

Candidates who have successfully completed B Sc Electrical can also apply for lateral entry to 2nd year ofB. Tech Electrical Distance Education Course.

Eligible candidates must be employed/ self-employed in order to pursueB. Tech Electrical through Distance Education.

Advantages ofB. Tech Electrical Distance Education

The term “electricity” became the subject of interest for various scientists in 17th century. Its origin is not as old as that of mechanical engineering or civil engineering. With some significant developments in 19th century, electric engineering started to evolve rapidly and has come a long way till now. Electricity being the soul of almost every industry, qualified Electrical Engineers are highly in demand by all sectors of engineering industry.

B. Tech Electrical Degree is required to be called as an Electrical Engineer. DistanceB. Tech Electric Engineering have advantages of saving money and time. Aspirants who cannot afford Regular On-campusB. Tech program, due to finance, can prefer joiningB. Tech Electrical Engineering Distance Education.B. Tech Electrical Distance Education is a blessing for working professionals not able to attend Regular programs. DistanceB. Tech helps them to complete their electrical degree without leaving their job. PursuingB. Tech Electrical Engineering through distance mode increase the chances of placement for freshers. It also helps already engaged working professionals to gain promotion. Self-employed individuals are also be benefitted byB. Tech Electrical Distance Education. They can successfully complete theirB. Tech Mechanical through Distance Education and obtain “Electrical Licence” required for getting electrical contracts.

Career opportunities afterB. Tech Mechanical Distance Education

Electrical Engineering plays a vital role in our day today’s life. Electricity being the energy behind almost every machine operating in the world. Electrical engineers are highly required in wide range of industries like telecommunication, electronics, Building, RF engineering, mobile technologies, computer engineering, etc. After completingB. Tech Electrical Engineering Distance Education, One can:

  • Start a job as an Electrical engineer, Electrical Safety Officer or Marine Engineer in various industries like automobile, construction, power & energy, oil & gas, shipping industries.
  • Purse master degrees like MBA, M. Tech Mechanical, in reputed and recognized universities, in India & abroad.
  • Start a job as Electrical engineer, Project Managers, Design Engineer, Quality Controller, Maintenance Engineer etc. in top Mechanical companies in India & abroad.
  • Get a job as Electrical Engineer, Electrical Design Consultant, Senior Engineer etc. in Public Sector Undertakings like BHEL, ONGC, BHABHA, PGCIL etc.
  • Join Government Departments like Electric Board, PWD, Indian Railway, Army, Navy, Air Force, etc.
  • Start your own businesses like electrical design & consultancy firms, electrical equipment supplier, electrical circuit or components manufacturer, etc.
