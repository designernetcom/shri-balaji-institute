Welcome to Shri Balaji Institute Pune (SBIP)

Welcome to the website of Shri Balaji Institute Pune (SBIP). Shri Balaji Institute Pune SBIP is a dedicated Distance Education advisory and consulting firm situated at Chinchwad, Pune, Maharashtra, India. We are committed to deliver best distance education consultancy services through strategic association with internationally accredited universities.

It is one of the best distance education consulting institutes in the city. Shri Balaji Institute Pune (SBIP) was established in 2013 with a vision to provide a second chance to those who, because of various reasons, cannot complete their education. Now, with more expertise and experience in distance education, SBIP serves people with incomplete education and working professionals who want to upgrade their academics. Our services include distance education from secondary level to post-graduate level, online courses, career counselling, and career guidance.
