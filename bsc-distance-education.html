About B. Sc Distance Education

B. Sc Distance Education is a one the most considerable popular 3 years undergraduate science degree program. B. Sc is an short form for Bachelor of Science program. B. Sc can be general classified into two categories: B. Sc General and B. Sc Honors. twain the programs are of three years duration. B. Sc common produces a broad basic knowledge of a group of multi-disciplinary subjects (like PCM, PCB, ZBC) of science. B. Sc General program do not offer specialization in a particular science field. However, B. Sc Honors offers large knowledge of a specific science field.

B. Sc program is designed to offer solid theoretical and practical understanding of various scientific fields such as physics, chemistry, mathematics, biochemistry, zoology, etc. B. Sc, both general & honors, can be pursued in regular and distance learning mode.

Distance B. Sc programs are blessings for those who cannot take orderly classes because of any cause. B.Sc Distance education permits the candidate to carry on their education in science field, without having to worry about their jobs. Distance Education B. Sc is for those, 12th science, candidates who have no desire to swap their sides, to commerce or arts, under any circumstances. There are not very universities in India offering this course through Distance/ Correspondence/ Online learning method.

Age-Limit for B. Sc Distance Education

There is no upper age-limit for pursuing B. Sc course through Distance education.

Objectives of B. SC Distance Education

To enable aspirants, who cannot take regular classroom program, in pursuing the course through distance mode.

It enables, students & working professionals, to work and learn at the same time.

To offer the program at a very affordable price.

To offer various B. Sc program through Distance/ Correspondence mode.

To provide comprehensive theoretical & practical knowledge of opted science stream.

To develop communication & management skills that will help them to prosper in their career.

Career options after B. Sc Distance Education

As Science is everywhere, there is no shortage of jobs for science aspirants. According to the opted streams, Distance B. Sc graduates can get jobs in various sectors such as Schools, Pharmaceutical Industry, Healthcare Industry, Laboratories, Forest Departments, Oil Industry, Sewage Treatment Plant, Chemical Industry, etc.
