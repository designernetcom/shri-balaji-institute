About BCA Distance Education

BCA Distance Education is a 3 years undergraduate computer degree program which deals with the study of computers and information technology. BCA is an abbreviation for Bachelor of Computer Application. This course is for those who are fascinated about learning and interpreting the languages used and understood by computers. BCA is considered equivalent to B Tech Computer Engineering program in terms of the curriculum and knowledge imparted. This program is focused on delivering complete knowledge about computers, working of computers, computer programs and use of computers and computer programs. This course teaches from the fundaments of computers to its real-life application.

BCA can be pursued in both regular and distance mode. Distance BCA is an UGC-DEB approved program, having equal value & demand like Regular BCA. Distance BCA program is for those, students & working professionals, who cannot take up a regular classroom program.

Distance BCA is a career-oriented program for those who want to pursue a career in computer & IT related field. Distance BCA program imparts essential knowledge required to prepare an industry-ready computer expert, fully prepared to make a career in IT industry.

There are numerous universities in India offering BCA through Distance Education/ Correspondence/ Online Mode.

Eligibility for BCA Distance Education

12th Pass students of any stream are eligible for taking admission in Distance BCA.

Candidates having Diploma in Computer Engineering are also eligible for admission into Distance BCA.

Objectives of BCA Distance Education

To enable students, who cannot take regular classroom program, in pursuing the course through distance mode.

To help working professionals in pursuing the course without having to leave their jobs.

To make the course available at very low fees.

To provide complete knowledge about computers; from basics to advance.

To acquaint students with modern tools & techniques trending in IT industry.

To develop communication & management skills that will help them to prosper in their career.

Career options after BCA Distance Education

We are living in the era of digitalisation. Digitalisation has become an inherent part of our life. Computers and its applications have touched almost every aspect of our life. The Information Technology industry in India is growing rapidly. It has become very important for every industry to integrate latest IT trends in their business, in order to function effectively and efficiently. Thus, creating a plethora of opportunities for skilled & qualified IT professionals. Candidates pursing Distance BCA have various career options after completing their graduation:

  • Self-Employment: Dedicated & qualified aspirants can start their own business in IT field like website designing & development, software development, etc.
  • Students can get jobs in reputed IT companies like IBM, Infosys, Wipro, etc.
  • They can take up roles such as web developer, system engineer, software tester, software developer, etc.
  • IT professional are required in Banks, Schools & other organizations, as a system administrator, for managing the system or server of the organization
  • Government departments like Indian Army, Airforce, Railway, etc. recruit IT professionals.
  • They can also take up jobs, like IT engineers, in public sector undertakings like ONGC, NTPC, etc.
  • After completing the course students can opt for higher education degrees like MBA, MCA in reputed institutes like IIM, Pune University, etc.
