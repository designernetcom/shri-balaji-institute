About One Sitting Degree

"One Sitting Degree" is a program that helps to bridge the academic gap years of a candidate. It is a discontinued way of acquiring Graduation or Post-Graduation Degree in short frame of time, instead of waiting for prescribed duration of the degree. [One Sitting Degree] helps individual, who had to discontinue their education after 12th, to obtain a degree in minimum period of time. [One Sitting Degree] is yet another enormously searched program over the internet. It is also known as “Fast-Track Degree Program”. This high volume of search shows that there are huge number of individuals are requiring One Sitting Degree. Many, UGC-AIU-AICTE-DEB approved, universities are offering "One Sitting Degree". The universities offer, both Graduation & Post-Graduation under this One Sitting Degree Program.

Is One Sitting Degree Valid?

Till the mid 90’s, One Sitting Degree was, both technically & practically valid. But in the year 1997, One Sitting Degree was discontinued and declared invalid as per UGC notice dated 04/03/1997. This made issuing One Sitting Degrees invalid for universities.

However, there are many UGC-AICTE-AIU-DEB approved universities offering One Sitting Degrees. Degrees issued in One Sitting are also verified by these universities. It might be helping lots of students in securing good jobs, promotions and further education. But technically, it is not allowed to issue Degrees in One Sitting.

Note: Shri Balaji Institute Pune SBIP do not promote or encourage One Sitting Degree Programs. This article is published to create awareness regarding One Sitting Degree. SBIP offers proper guidance and alternate valid solutions to individuals seeking One Sitting Degree.

Benefits of One Sitting Degree

Today is the era of high competition. In today’s world, merely passing high school is not going to help to thrive. Having education beyond high school have become very important to survive in this mercilessly competitive world. Taking this scenario into account, the individuals who were not able to purse their degree (Graduation/ Post-Graduation) are totally helpless. They are desperately looking for options to complete their Degree in One Sitting. So, that they too can start their career confidently. Although UGC had declared it invalid to issue One Sitting Degree, there are several UGC approved universities providing Degrees through One Sitting mode. One Sitting Degree benefits individuals in several way such as:

  • Securing jobs in top Private Ltd. companies & MNC’s.
  • Securing jobs in Public Ltd. Companies & Government Sector.
  • Pursuing further education in any recognized university/ institute.
  • Appearing competitive exams like UPSC, MPSC, Banking Exams, etc.
  • Pursuing education in foreign countries.
  • Getting jobs in reputed companies located in foreign countries.

FAQs about One Sitting Degree

Q - Is One Sitting Degree Valid in India?

No. UGC had discontinued One Sitting Degree and declared it invalid for universities to issue Degrees in One Sitting.


Q - Is there any UGC approved university providing One Sitting Degree?

Yes. There are several universities offerings One Sitting Degree.


Q - Is One Sitting Degree valid for government jobs?

One Sitting Degrees offered by UGC approved universities are only valid for Govt. jobs.


Q - Can I appear public service commission exams after One Sitting Degree?

Yes. You can appear UPSC or any other state public service exams, if the Degree is secured from an UGC approved university.


Q - Is One Sitting Degree valid for abroad studies or job?

Yes. One Sitting Degrees issued by UGC approved universities are definitely valid.


Q - Is this program valid for further education?

Yes. You can take admission into any recognized university/ institute.
