About BBA Distance Education

BBA Distance Education is one of the most popular undergraduate programs. It is a three-years management degree that develops entrepreneurship & business administration skills in students. BBA stands for Bachelor of Business Administration; as the name suggests this program imparts knowledge required to administer the proper functioning of any business.

BBA is a general program that, covers and offers fundamental knowledge of all topics related to business management such as organizational behavior, management skills, business strategy, marketing, market trends, business communication, competition, law related to business, business ethics, financial accounting, entrepreneurship development, finance, retail management, supply chain management, capital management, etc. However, there are many universities offering Honors program in BBA. Honors program in BBA are drafted in a way to cover basic aspects of all management subjects and exclusively focus on a particular management stream.

Both, general & honors, programs of BBA can be pursed in either full-time mode or part-time mode (distance/ correspondence/ online). Distance BBA is a perfect option for those who cannot pursue regular BBA program owing to various personal or professional reasons. It allows, students & working professionals, to complete their management graduation without actually going to colleges. Distance BBA is equivalent to Regular BBA program in terms of weightage, demand and curriculum. It prepares industry-ready managers by imparting detailed theoretical knowledge of business management. There are several UGC-AIU-DEB approved universities in India offering BBA Distance Education program.

Eligibility Criteria to take Admission in BBA Distance Program

The candidate must have passed the 12th class from any stream.

Those who have completed a Diploma (3 Years) are also eligible for taking admission in Distance BBA.

BBA Distance Education Fees

Age Limit for BBA Distance Education

There is no upper age-limit for pursuing this program.

Objectives of Distance BBA

To offer flexibility to the students to pursue the classes whenever and wherever they want.

To allow students to learn managerial skills and business administration at their own pace.

To offer the program at very affordable fees.

To enable working professionals to complete the business management education, without leaving their jobs.

To impart good managerial and communication skills in students.

To develop good leadership skills in students required to operate any company or organization.

To impart business ethics and professionalism in students.

To impart the confidence of working in team environment.
