About M. TECH DISTANCE EDUCATION

M. TECH DISTANCE EDUCATION or ME Distance Education is a 2 years postgraduate engineering degree program for students who want to continue their studies in technology and work as researcher in the field of technology, after completing their bachelor’s degree in technology. M. Tech & ME stands for Master of Technology & Master of Engineering respectively. M.Tech Distance Learning imparts in-depth knowledge of a particular subject belonging to the engineering branch opted by students during their bachelors. of a specific engineering branch. It offers exceptional knowledge & research of a specific subject and moulds the learner into a specialist of the subject. Research & Thesis are two important part of this course. M. Tech can be pursued in both Regular & Distance Learning mode. M. TECH Distance education helps eligible candidates and working professionals an opportunity to continue their technical education. There are several universities in India offering M Tech through Distance Learning/ Correspondence/ Online/ Part-Time mode.

Is B. TECH through distance education valid?

Yes, M Tech through Distance Education/ Part-Time/ Correspondence mode is approved by UGC-DEB. There are many universities in India offering this course through distance education.

Eligibility for M. Tech Distance Education

Students having B. Tech degree are eligible to take admission in relevant field of M. Tech.

Students having M. Sc in Science/ Mathematics/ Computer Science/ Electricals/ etc are eligible to take admission in relevant field of M. Tech.

Students with MCA can also apply for admission to relevant computer engineering branches of M. Tech.

Fees for M. Tech Distance Education

50K – 80K Per Year (As fees varies according to universities)

Objectives of M. Tech Distance Education

To enable working professionals in pursuing postgraduate engineering degree without bothering their jobs.

To make this course accessible to the aspirants from the comfort of their home.

To impart extensive knowledge regarding specific branch of engineering.

To develop communication skills which can help them to excel in their career.

To impart comprehensive interdisciplinary knowledge of the opted engineering field.

To equip the aspirants with leadership & management skills helping them to perform well in a team environment.

Career options after M. Tech Distance Education

B. Tech is sufficient to secure a decent job in engineering field but having a topping of M. Tech degree to it, open doors to wide range of career options.

M. Tech degree holders are offered higher salary packages as compared to candidates only having engineering graduation degree.

M. Tech candidates can purse Research program (Ph. D) for more deeper knowledge.

Aspirants interested in teaching can start their career as teachers or can appear UGC NET exam to become lecturers at reputed university departments.

M. Tech candidates can join top corporate companies as Researchers, Software Developers, Project Manager, etc.

One can also join Public sector companies likes BHEL, NTPC etc.
