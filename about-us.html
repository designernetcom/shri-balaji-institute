WHO WE ARE

With more than 6 years’ experience in the education sector, Shri Balaji Institute Pune (SBIP) offers courses from Secondary level to Post Graduation level via regular and distance mode. We assist candidates to choose and enrol from 200+ courses, available in different modes, as per their choice and convenience. As an organization, we serve as a point of contact for the regular and distant education programs offered by various boards and universities.

Shri Balaji Institute Pune (SBIP) provides learning material for almost every course available. Our learning material, for many of the regular and distance programmes, is a blend of books and online learning resources. We provide the most modern and quality learning material to ensure that the learning is practically oriented and easy to follow. Thus, allowing learners to learn at their own pace and consume varied content, such as, documents, presentations, videos and more.
