AboutB. Tech Mechanical Distance Education

B Tech Distance Education in Mechanical Engineering is a 4 years/ 3 years (B Tech Mechanical Lateral Entry) degree program. Mechanical engineering is one of the oldest, widest, versatile and everlasting branches of engineering. It not only covers physics & mathematics, but also contains important aspects of almost every field of engineering.B. Tech Distance Mechanical engineering teaches the combined application of physics, mathematics & material science to understand, examine, design, manufacture and maintain various mechanical systems. It is present, some way or the other, in every product or service existing in today’s modern world. To be a Mechanical Engineer, you need to acquire basic knowledge of all branches of engineering.B. Tech Distance Education in Mechanical Engineering course is designed to shape a person, into a skilled and versatile individual, who is able to analyse the constituents required to develop technologies, in a cost-efficient manner, to meet the human needs in modern times.B. Eligibility forB. Tech Mechanical Distance Education

Aspirants having 10+2 in Science with Physics, Chemistry & Mathematics as subjects are eligible forB. Tech Mechanical Distance Education Course.

Diploma Mechanical Engineering (3 Years) holders are eligible for lateral entry to 2nd year ofB. Tech Mechanical Distance Education Course.

Eligible candidates must be employed/ self-employed in order to pursueB. Tech Mechanical through Distance Education.

Advantages ofB. Tech Mechanical Distance Education

The existence of Mechanical engineering techniques can be traced back to the early human civilizations. But, the real need of mechanical engineering emerged, in 18th century, when industrial revolution started. With the development in the field of physics, Mechanical engineering techniques evolved rapidly. Mechanical Engineering is the top field of engineering. Mechanical Engineers, being versatile in nature, are highly in demand by all sectors of engineering industry.

To secure a decent job as a Mechanical Engineer in engineering industry, one must possessB. Tech Mechanical Degree. Candidates wanting to enter mechanical industry without aB. Tech Mechanical degree are not able to get good job. Candidates who are already engaged in the Mechanical Industry without a B. Tech Degree are not able to excel in their career. High school pass candidates who cannot take up regular on-campus program, due to financial constraints and family obligations, can take upB. Tech Mechanical Distance Education. DistanceB. Tech fees is very low as compared to the RegularB. Tech program. DistanceB. Tech Mechanical course also aids to individuals, who are already working, to pursue further education in distance mode.B. Self-employed individuals can also be benefitted byB. Tech Mechanical Distance Education. They can successfully complete theirB.
